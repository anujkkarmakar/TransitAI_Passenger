Guide

How to Use TransitAI Passenger

Complete guide to getting started and making the most of TransitAI Passenger

1

Installation and Setup

System Requirements:

  • Android 12 or higher
  • Minimum 2GB RAM
  • 100MB free storage
  • Active internet connection
  • Location services enabled

Installation Steps:

  • Download the APK from GitHub releases
  • Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in settings
  • Install the application
  • Grant required permissions when prompted
2

Account Creation and Login

New User Registration:

  • Open the app and click "Register"
  • Enter your name, email, and password
  • Verify your email address
  • Complete your profile with additional details

Existing User Login:

  • Enter your registered email
  • Enter your password
  • Use "Forgot Password" if needed
3

Booking Your Ticket

Booking Process:

  • Select "Book Ticket" from the main menu
  • Choose your departure and destination stops
  • Select journey type (One Way/Return)
  • Choose bus type (AC/Non-AC)
  • View fare calculation
  • Enter mobile number
  • Confirm booking

You'll receive a confirmation email with ticket details and QR code

4

Using Your Ticket

During Your Journey:

  • Access ticket from "My Tickets" section
  • Show QR code to conductor for verification
  • Keep ticket active screen accessible
  • Ensure phone has sufficient battery

Pro Tip: Download PDF version for offline access

5

Managing Your Bookings

Ticket Management:

  • View all tickets in "My Tickets" section
  • Check active and expired tickets
  • Download ticket PDF
  • Cancel tickets (within 4 hours of booking)
  • View booking history

Track your environmental impact through the dashboard

6

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions:

QR Code Not Scanning

  • Increase screen brightness
  • Clean phone screen
  • Ensure good lighting

Booking Failed

  • Check internet connection
  • Verify payment details
  • Try again after few minutes
